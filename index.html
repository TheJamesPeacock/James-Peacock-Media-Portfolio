About Me

My name is James Peacock. I am a 20 year old coding student with a passion for film-making and photography. My passion for film started back in 2015, when I got the chance to experience the entire process of film-making on a set of a show and saw how everything worked behind the scenes, I've been in love with it ever since. This past year I decided I wanted to pick up a skill that I could use to create more things and what better way to do that other than coding. I've only just started learning whenever I signed up for Georgia Tech's Development Bootcamp but everyday, I enjoy it more and more and I learn something new. In no time I'm going to be better than I ever thought possible and it starts with this website. Overtime I'm going to keep updating this website, making it look better and feel better. I'm also going to keep it updated with anything I work on, whether it's something to do with coding or one of my other passions like photography or film.
